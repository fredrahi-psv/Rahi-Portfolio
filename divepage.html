'DIVE! A SEA OPERA' - Videography, Album Art, and Art Direction/Conceptualization

'DIVE! A SEA OPERA' is an EP that I made with talented musician/producer (and close friend) DiLL. The EP features oceanic inspiration throughout its production and lyrical content, and draws heavily from my personal relationship with the ocean.

I leaned into my past a lot on this EP, and the settings for the videography are locations where I spent a lot of time in my childhood. The 'DIVE!' shortform video, for example, was filmed in The Entrance, NSW, a place my family and I stayed multiple times a year up until my late teens.

I filmed everything on a SONY CCD TRV58 Hi8 Camcorder, and the photos were taken on a Canon KODAK PIXPRO AZ528 and a SONY DSC-H90.
