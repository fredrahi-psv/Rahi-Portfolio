CityHub Internship

I interned as a writer at CityHub as part of my Macquarie University PACE unit in semester 2 of 2024. I was tasked with writing a news article and an arts and entertainment article each week. The articles were all published online and four of them made it to print in the CityHub monthly magazine.

During my internship, I learned to engage with different audiences through diverse content and polished my storytelling and editing skills. This experience also provided insights into the editorial process and collaboration within a publishing team.

I am proud to have contributed to CityHub's mission of providing relevant and engaging content, which has helped shape my path toward a career in journalism and media.
